Architectural Concepts & Philosophy

1. The AI Core & FinOps

SLMs (The "Fast Edge")

  • Purpose: High-speed bouncers handling high-volume tasks in milliseconds.
  • Action: Parses logs, flags single obfuscated CLI commands instantly, and masks PII/PHI before downstream storage.

Agentic LLMs (The "Deep Brain")

  • Purpose: Act as Tier-3 Senior Analysts evaluating high-fidelity "Alerts".
  • Action: Correlates disparate events across time, analyzes malware logic, and formulates surgical mitigation strategies.

Engineering Lens

Observability & Resilience

If Kafka queues fill up or vLLM GPUs max out, the SOC is blind. A dedicated Observability stack (Prometheus/Grafana) ensures internal health is monitored continuously.

GitOps & IaC

Changes to SOC logic are pushed deterministically via GitOps (ArgoCD) to prevent configuration drift and allow instant rollbacks of broken correlation rules.

Enterprise Arch Lens

Multi-Cloud CNAPP

The enterprise spans AWS, Azure, and GCP. A CNAPP platform provides deep context into cloud control planes, identities, and serverless architectures.

iPaaS Integration Bus

Point-to-point APIs are an anti-pattern. The AI uses an Enterprise Service Bus (MuleSoft/Boomi) to provide standardized connectors to corporate tools.

GRC & Compliance Lens

Data Sovereignty

A Sovereignty Router explicitly evaluates data origin and routes EU logs to geo-fenced LLM enclaves to prevent massive GDPR fines.

Policy-as-Code (PaC)

PaC engines (OPA) evaluate every AI action against strict digital policies before execution, acting as a hard programmatic brake on unauthorized actions.
